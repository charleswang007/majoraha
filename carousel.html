The problems

It is often difficult for freshmen and sophomore college students in the US to make an informed decision about which major to focus on until graduation. They are usually given an incomplete overview of limited majors and thus cannot tackle the major selection problem rationally enough to make a non-regrettable choice. Therefore, the answer to this problem is simply putting convincing data publicly available in front of the end-users, in a cogent, concise, and informative manner.

For who?

If you are undergraduate students whose majors are undecided are the potential users. Our product will be very useful for you! We provide an in-depth view of various aspects (salary, career development, and social networks) of all the available majors offered by their institutions .
